OCTOBER-DECEMBER 2023

ECG DEVICE DEVELOPMENT WITH COMPUTER INTERFACE

Conducted research on ECG circuits and designed a functional circuit using Proteus software. Led the development of a printed circuit board (PCB) and designed the overall device, including a user interface implemented through LabVIEW, to enable effective data acquisition and monitoring.

APRIL-MAY 2024

MARINE RESCUE DRONE AND PATIENT GPS TRACKING (PROJECT: S.O.M.S.A.K.)

Designed to improve rescue operations for unexpected drowning incidents, this project uses an Arduino-powered sensor and tracking system. The drone detects a signal emitted by a bracelet worn by the user, allowing it to navigate directly to the individual in distress. Upon reaching the location, the drone creates a secure support area, enabling the person to hold on and swim back to safety independently.

MAY 2024- PRESENT

RESEARCH ASSISTANT IN SOFT ROBOTIC ACTUATOR FOR FINGER REHABILITATION.

Developed MATLAB scripts to simulate kinematics of robotic fingers, optimizing workspace design by analyzing joint angles (theta) and intersect areas. Using CAD software to design and fabricate molds for soft actuators, leading to the successful construction of functional prototypes. Contributed to the integration of simulation data with physical design to enhance actuator performance and usability.

FEBRURARY-APRIL 2023

2ND RUNNER-UP IN PRE-INCUBATION FOR DELTA CUP 2023

Participate in Delta smart & green industrial automation contest 2023 in the topic of designing system for collecting emitted Carbon dioxide gas to reinforce the concrete by CO2 mineralization methods.

By using my CAD design with research journal analyses combined with planning business idea for cement industry, The pitched idea made our team got the 2nd place in the pre-incubation round.
